Our Facilities

Highly Educated & Qualified Faculty. Equipped with Best Library in the Region. Fully Wi-Fi Campus.

University Library

The University Library, named as Bhai Kahn Singh Nabha Library, is housed in a spacious modern and Air-Conditioned, Wi-Fi enabled building. The library stocks more than 5.64 lacs volumes and subscribes to 290 print journals. Latest books are added every year.

Play Grounds

Air-conditioned Shooting Range (10 Meter), Standard Boxing Hall, IAAF Synthetic Athletics Track, Velodrome (for cycling) with flood lights and beautiful pavilion. Two standard outdoor concrete Basketball courts. One Hockey field (grassy). Three Volleyball courts with enclosure. Two Football field (grassy). One open Archery range of 100 Mts. Indoor Gymnasium Hall

Hostels

There are six residential Hostels for boys and seven residential Hostels for girls. The Hostels have all modern amenities for comfortable living for 4000 students. All the Hostels have wi-fi facility & CCTV Camera
